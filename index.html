Why This Collab

This is about legacy, identity, and building something that feels like New York—through my lens.

From the Carolinas → Looking at the Knicks

As a kid in the Carolinas, watching New York felt like watching a different universe. The Knicks had energy, grit, and style — it wasn’t just basketball, it was culture.

What Patrick Ewing Means to Me

Patrick Ewing represented strength, leadership, and responsibility. He made Madison Square Garden feel like the center of the league. This collab is my salute to that era.

What We’re Building

A full drop ecosystem: the shoe, special box, custom tissue, a collectible UFO basketball, plus a mobile-first AR layer to unlock story + digital assets.
